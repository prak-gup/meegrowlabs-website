Meegrow Social

Meegrow Social is Meegrow Labs' social media management tool. Plan, schedule, publish, and analyze content across your connected social accounts — including Facebook Pages, Instagram, Google Business Profiles, YouTube, LinkedIn, and X — all from one calendar.

What it does

Schedule & publish

Draft posts and videos once, schedule them, and publish to the accounts you connect at the right time.

One content calendar

See everything planned across every connected channel in a single view.

Analytics

Track how content published through Meegrow Social performs, in one dashboard.

Multi-channel

Connect Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, X and more, and manage them together.

Connecting your Facebook Page & Instagram account

When you connect a Facebook Page, Meegrow Social uses the Meta Platform to publish the posts you schedule to your Page, read your Page's posts and engagement for your content calendar, and show you Page insights for content you published through us. If your Page has a linked Instagram Business account, you can connect that too — we read its profile and media for your calendar, publish the content you schedule to it, and show you its insights. You must own or be authorised to manage any Page or account you connect, and we only act on your behalf to do what you explicitly schedule or request.

Read how we handle data, and the exact permissions we request and why, in our Privacy Policy. Your use of Meegrow Social is subject to our Terms of Service, and your use of Facebook and Instagram features is also subject to the Meta Terms of Service and the Meta Privacy Policy. You can revoke access anytime at facebook.com/settings?tab=business_tools, or by disconnecting the channel in Meegrow Social — see our Data Deletion page.

Connecting your YouTube channel

When you connect a YouTube channel, Meegrow Social uses YouTube API Services to publish the videos you schedule, read your channel and existing videos for your content calendar, and show you analytics for content you published through us. We only access this data to provide the features you ask for.

Read how we handle data in our Privacy Policy. Your use of YouTube features is also subject to the YouTube Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy. You can revoke access anytime at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
